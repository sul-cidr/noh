    <h2>Back Circlet (<em>mawari biraki</em>)</h2>

    <p>The Back circlet (<em>mawari biraki</em>) starts with the dancer facing backstage. A <a href="/movement/step-pivot" target="_blank">Right Step pivot (<em>migi kakeru</em>)</a> is then concluded with an <a href="/movement/open-retreat" target="_blank">Open-retreat (<em>hiraki</em>)</a>.It is often used to conclude a <a href="/movement/large-circling-l" target="_blank">Left circling (<em>hidari ≈çmawari</em>)</a>.</p>
