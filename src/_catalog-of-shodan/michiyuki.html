The michiyuki is a kind of ageuta. Also set in hiranori, its overall melodic trajectory compares well with the ageuta's. Its text describes a traveler's journey or his emotions and usually leads to the entrance music of the shite.

There is no michiyuki in neither Hashitomi nor Kokaji.
