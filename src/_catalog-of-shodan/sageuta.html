Catalog of Shōdan: Main Chants

Sageuta

Voice
Congruent
Percussion
Strict

A short song with a melody centered on Medium and Low tones . Based on poetic text with regular rhythm set in hiranori, it is often preceded by a Sashi, and leads to an Ageuta. In Hashitomi’s case, it leads to an Ageuta but it is not preceded by a Sashi. {% include video-no-background.html src="http://d7rcwrflqckpu.cloudfront.net/Shodan_sl/Hashitomi-Sageuta-Score_sl.mp4" %}
