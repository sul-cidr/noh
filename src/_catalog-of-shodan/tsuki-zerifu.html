Catalog of Shōdan: Spoken

Tsuki-zerifu

Voice
Unmetered

This unaccompanied spoken shōdan> is typically delivered by a traveling waki to confirm his arrival at a destination. It often follows a travel song known as the michiyuki.

Examples in the Plays:

Takasago - Tsuki-zerifu
