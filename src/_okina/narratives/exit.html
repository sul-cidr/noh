The performers and stage property leave the stage.

The exit of the actors and musicians happens in silence. The shite is the first one to exit, followed by the waki. If a tsukurimono was used in the play, the stage assistants would then remove it. Once the actor or stage assistant has passed the Second pine, the curtain is lifted and then pulled back down once he has entered the anteroom. From this point, the musicians exit in the same order they entered, nokhan player first, followed by the kotsuzumi, ≈çtsuzumi,and taiko players. Upon reaching the Second pine, the curtain is pulled back partially on one side. The jiutai members exit through the back left side door, starting with the back row. Even as they are exiting the stage, actors, assistants and musicians exhibit great modesty and decorum. Thus, the audience holds its applause until the last musician exits.
