First Act - Shite Enters:
Monologue
Synopsis
Munechika, mindful of the difficulty of the task ahead, goes to pray for help to the family deity at the Inari Shrine.
Text
Prose.
Music
Unaccompanied vocal part of the waki delivered in kotoba.
Visuals
The waki stands up and walks to Square 1 to deliver his monologue facing the audience. He then directs himself toward Square 6 on his way to the Inari Shrine.

The intermedia texture is extremely simple. The waki stands up and walks to deliver a monologue from Square 1, the most common and quietest position used by an actor for a first address to the audience.

In an inconspicuous way, it is this unmetered and unaccompanied monologue that will provide the shite with a reason to enter. More specifically, it is by stating that he will seek divine help that the swordsmith has set up the context for the deity's appearance.
