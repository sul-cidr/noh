Form
Second Act - Waki Waits:
Machiutai
Synopsis
The priest and his two attendants travel on boat and arrive at Suminoe.
Text
Poetry in regular rhythm.
Music
The joint chant of waki and wakizure set in hiranori is sung in tsuyogin style. It is accompanied by the percussion in strict and nohkan in non-congruent rhythmic settings. In the final measure they are joined by the taiko drum.
Visuals
The two wakizure stand up, walk to Square 2 and 3 and turn to face the waki, who stood up at Square 5. After finishing the chant, they return to their previous seating positions.
