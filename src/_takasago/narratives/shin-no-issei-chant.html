First Act - Shite Enters:
Shin no Issei Music → Shin no Issei Chant → Sashi 1 → Sageuta → Ageuta 1
Synopsis
The old man and old woman comment on the spring wind blowing through the pines and the sound of the waves gently reaching the shore.
Text
Poetic text in regular rhythm.
Music
This non-congruent chant, sung in a large part by shite and shitezure together, is performed in einori, is sung in the high register using tsuyogin style. It is accompanied by a non-congruent nohkan part with percussion in strict setting.
Visuals
The two actors face each other standing at the 3rd and 1st Pines. They comment jointly on the gentle wind blowing through the pines as this spring day draws to a close. They then turn to the audience for the ninoku, during which the tsure sings about the waves reaching the shore. Once again facing each other, they reflect together on the regular, cyclical movement of ocean water as it ebbs and flows. The shōdan concludes with an Ashirai, as both actors enter the stage; the tsure moves to Square 6, while the shite goes to Square 1.
