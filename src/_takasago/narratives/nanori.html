Synopsis
The waki introduces himself as a priest coming from the Aso Shrine in Higo Province, Kyushu. He explains that he and his two attendants are on the way to the capital and plan to stop at Takasago Bay in Banshū along the way.

Prose.

Visuals
The waki stands at Square 8 and only turns slightly for a short moment in the middle of the monologue.

The monk introduces himself and explains his intentions in the simplest way, by speaking unaccompanied. The speech is tranquil, steady, and performed almost without movement while the actor remains on Square 8. When sharing his decision to travel he briefly turns slightly to the right and then back.
