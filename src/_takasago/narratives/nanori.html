Netori Okitsuzumi → Kaikō → Nanori → Shidai → Michiyuki → Tsukizerifu
Synopsis
The priest introduces himself as coming from the Aso Shrine in Higo Province, Kyushu. He explains that he and his two attendants are on the way to the capital and plan to stop at Takasago Bay in Banshū along the way.
Text
Prose.
Music
Unaccompanied vocal part of the waki delivered in kotoba.
Visuals
The waki stands at Square 9 and only turns slightly for a short moment in the middle of the monologue.

CHeck if this is useful......The monk introduces himself and explains his intentions in the simplest way, by speaking unaccompanied. The speech is tranquil, steady, and performed almost without movement while the actor remains on Square 9. Although the overall texture remains simple, the inclusion of the narrative that answers the 'who,' 'why,' 'what' and 'where' about the monk, gives this shōdan a higher Intermedia Index than the preceding Nanoribue.
