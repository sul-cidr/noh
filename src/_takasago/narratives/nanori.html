Netori Okitsuzumi → Kaikō → Nanori → Shidai chant → Michiyuki → Tsuki-zerifu
Synopsis
The waki introduces himself as a priest coming from the Aso Shrine in Higo Province of Kyushu. He explains that he and his two attendants are on the way to the capital but plan to visit Takasago Bay in Banshū along the way.
Text
Prose.
Music
Unaccompanied vocal part of the waki delivered in kotoba.
Visuals
The waki stands at Square 8 and only turns slightly for a short moment in the middle of the monologue.

The priest introduces himself and states his intentions plainly, speaking without accompaniment. His delivery is tranquil and steady, with almost no movement, as he remains on Square 8. When he declares his decision to travel, he underscores it with a brief turn to the right before returning to face forward.
