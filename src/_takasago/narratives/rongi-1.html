First Act - Shite Exits:
Rongi 1
Synopsis
Dialogue between the old couple, answering in unison the jiutai’s questions who speaks for the priest. In the exchange, the old couple identify itself as "… Takasago and Suminoe’s paired pine gods appearing before you here as man and wife."
Text
Poetry in regular rhythm.
Music
Congruent chant set in hiranori, sang in tsuyogin style. It is supported by a non-congruent nohkan part, and a percussion part set in strict setting.
Visuals
The shite kneeling center stage (Square 9), slides left to face the waki with the line “…all within the sovereign's land, will share forever in the glory of the reign.” When the jiutai, speaking for the shite, confirms that “To Sumiyoshi I will go now”, he stands up goes to front stage (Square 4) and performs two silent large steps to mimic his going to Sumiyoshi.
