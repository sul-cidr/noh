Form
First Act - Shite Enters:
Shin no Issei Music → Shin no Issei Chant → Sashi 1 → Sageuta → Ageuta 1
Synopsis
The old couple prepares to sweep the needles and dust beneath the Takasago tree.
Text
Poetic text in regular rhythm.
Music
This congruent chant sung together by shite and shitezure is set in hiranori. It is accompanied by kotsuzumi and ōtsuzumi in strict setting.
Visuals
There is no conspicuous movement of the actors. Facing each other while standing on Squares 1 and 6, the shite and shitezure sing together.

The Sageuta typically focuses on the main character's internal desires or intentions, leading to minimal movement and action, which may unfold in the following ageuta. A key element in this Sageuta is the final line about sweeping or raking under the tree, which holds particular significance in Takasago as it metaphorically references the gathering of poetry collections. This line is emphasized through a repetition called kaeshi.
