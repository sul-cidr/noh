Mondō → Kakeai → Ageuta 2 → Spoken
Synopsis
The spirit of Lady Yūgao re-starts reciting the poem with which Genji replied to Lady Yūgao.
Text
Poetry in regular rhythm.
Music
The einori chant performed by the shite is accompanied by the percussion instruments in strict rhythmic setting. The nohkan concludes the jonomai by performing its non-congruent cadential tome pattern over the waka's first honji.
Visuals
The shite remains almost motionless on Square 9.
