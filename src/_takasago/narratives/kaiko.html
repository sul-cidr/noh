Form
First Act - Waki Enters:
Netori Okitsuzumi → Kaikō → Nanori → Shidai chant → Michiyuki → Tsuki-zerifu
Synopsis
The waki delivers a text prepared especially for this occasion by Prof. Reiko Yamanaka. It informs that this performance is intended to commemorate the opening of the relocated Kyoto City University of Arts.
Text
Arhythmic poetry.
Music
Unaccompanied song of the waki set in sashinori and sung in tsuyogin style.
Visuals
The waki is standing at Square 8 as he delivers the monologue.

The Kaiko is a very rare shōdan in Noh, composed and performed only for special occasions. It is presented with great ceremony and respect at the fittingly central yet humble Square 8 of the stage.
