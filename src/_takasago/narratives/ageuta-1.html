Shin no Issei Music → Shin no Issei Chant → Sashi 1 → Sageuta → Ageuta 1
Synopsis
The old couple reflects on the question, 'How long will our lives continue?' The reference to the Live Pine of Iki reinforces the themes of longevity and enduring natural beauty, embodying the idea of a long and happy life.
Text
Poetic text in regular rhythm.
Music
This congruent chant sung together by shite and shitezure is set in hiranori. It is accompanied by a non-congruent nohkan part, and a percussion part in strict setting.
Visuals
Facing each other while standing on Squares 1 and 6, the old couple acknowledges the Takasago tree. They briefly turn to face the front, recognizing its old age, before turning to face each other again. The old man then walks to Square 8, while the old woman walks to Square 1, hands her broom to the stage attendant and moves to Square 2 facing front.
