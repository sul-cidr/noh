Ex. something about monochromatic costume gives an sense of subdued and subtle elegance.

The simple instrumentation of this unmetered melody, the absence of text, and the to-be-expected simple walk-in of the actor provides this shōdan with the play's lowest Intermedia Index, a common way of starting the first shōdan of a play’s first dan.

It is the nohkan player’s responsibility to loosely adapt the pacing of the melody to the waki’s walking speed so that the conclusion of the entrance music coincides with the waki’s final kata : the Step back.
