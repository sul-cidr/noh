Form
Second Act - Dialogue:
Sashi 3 → Jo-no-ei → Issei
Synopsis
Sumiyoshi's god reflects on his long devotion to the sacred pine of Sumiyoshi and the soothing nature of the nighttime dances accompanied by drums.
Text
Arhtyhmic poetry.
Music
The shite sings this sashinori chant, in tsuyogin style accompanied by the nohkan in non-congruent and three percussion instruments in strict rhythmic settings.
Visuals
The shite stands by Pine 1 to deliver his chant. At the conclusion, he performs a series of kata that signifies an increase in motion intensity, beginning with the opening of his fan and culminating in a Forward Point, which is finished with a swirling of his left sleeves.
