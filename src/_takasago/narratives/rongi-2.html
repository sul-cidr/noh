Synopsis
The beauty of the dance brings an association with the reign: “…where the essence of the piece is echoed in the 'Dance of Blue Ocean Waves.' The path of the gods and the sovereign intertwines and leads directly to Miyako, the capital in spring. This is exemplified in the dance 'Return to the Palace,' which symbolizes an endless and joyous reign.”
Text
Poetry in irregular rhythm.
Music
The shite and the juitai sing this congruent hiranori chant, in tsuyogin style accompanied by the nohkan in non-congruent and two drums in strict rhythmic settings.
Visuals
This is dance-to-text that is slower. The sequence of kata in the first half is performed on the stage’s left side. The dance’s second half gains in momentum as its sequence of kata takes advantage of the entire stage.
