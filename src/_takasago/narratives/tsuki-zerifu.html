Synopsis
The priest and the two attendants arrive at Takasago Bay in Harima province and express interest in the Takasago pine.
Text
Poetry in regular rhythm.
Music
Prose.
Visuals
The waki stands at Square 5 facing front, confirms that they have arrived at Takasago Bay. He then turns to face the two wakizure at Squares 2 and 3 as he suggests they look for a local person to learn about the place. Then, they move to the standard seating place in Squares 5 and 6.

The section concludes the travel to Takasago of the priest and the attendants and sets the stage for the introduction of the local characters.
