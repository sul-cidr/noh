Form
Second Act - Dialogue:
Sashi 3 → Jo-no-ei → Issei
Synopsis
The god of Sumiyoshi describes the lush greenery on the coast of Asaka.
Text
Poetic text in irregular rhythm.
Music
The shite and the juitai sing this non-congruent einori chant, in tsuyogin style accompanied by the nohkan in non-congruent and the three percussion instruments in strict rhythmic settings.
Visuals
The shite opens with a series of kata with a straight cross on the left side before moving to Square 8, where he initiates another series of kata with a straight cross central stage, concluding it with a sequence of stamps at Square 9, before returning to Square 8.
