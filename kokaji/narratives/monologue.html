Prose.

Visuals
The waki stands up and walks to Square 1 to deliver his monologue facing the audience. Then, he directs himself toward Square 6 on his way to the Inari Shrine.

The intermedia texture is extremely simple. The waki stands up and walks to deliver a monologue from Square 1, the most common and quietest position an actor uses to address the audience for the first time.

This unmetered and unaccompanied monologue provide the shite with an inconspicuous reason to enter. More specifically, by stating that he will seek divine help, the swordsmith sets up the context for the deity's appearance.
